🧠 Owner Dashboard – Post Approval (Zecoho)
Core Principle

Owners don’t care about features. They care about:

Are bookings coming?

How much money am I making?

What needs action right now?

Everything else is secondary.

1️⃣ Landing Screen (Owner Home)
Page title

“Owner Dashboard”
Subtext: Manage your property & bookings

🔥 Top KPI Strip (MOST IMPORTANT)

Show 4 cards only (not more):

[ Card 1 ]
📊 Bookings

Today

This month

[ Card 2 ]
💰 Revenue

₹ Today

₹ This month

[ Card 3 ]
🏨 Property Status

Live / Under Review / Suspended

[ Card 4 ]
⭐ Rating

Avg rating

No. of reviews

👉 These must be above the fold.

2️⃣ Property Quick Control Panel

A single card with high-impact actions:

Property Card

Property Name

City

Price per night

Status badge (🟢 Live / 🟡 Draft / 🔴 Action Required)

Buttons (ONLY THESE)

✏️ Edit Listing

📸 Upload Photos

💸 Change Price

🔒 Pause Listing

❌ Do NOT show:

Full address

Legal docs

KYC here

Messages Panel

Keep this light.

8️⃣ Settings (MINIMAL)

That’s it.

9️⃣ Permission Logic (VERY IMPORTANT)
Owner Status Types

Owner – Limited

Listing visible

Can receive inquiries

❌ Cannot accept payments

Owner – Verified

Full booking

Payouts enabled

Ranking boost

Owner – Suspended

Dashboard visible

Booking disabled

Red banner explaining why

10️⃣ What NOT to Build Now (Save Time)

❌ Multi-property analytics
❌ Yearly reports
❌ Discount engines
❌ Channel manager
❌ Tax reports

These kill MVP velocity.
