OWNER DASHBOARD — FIRST SCREEN (PRE-APPROVAL)
URL
/owner/dashboard


Triggered when:

user logged in

role = owner

status = pending / under_review / kyc_incomplete

1️⃣ TOP BANNER — STATUS FIRST (VERY IMPORTANT)

This must be the first thing they see.

Banner (full width, soft yellow or blue)
⏳ Your property is under review
Our team is verifying your details. This usually takes 24–48 hours.


If something is missing:

⚠️ Action required to go live
Please complete the highlighted steps below.


👉 This kills anxiety. Owners hate silence.

Title

Get Your Property Live (3 steps left)

Each item has:

Status icon

Short explanation

CTA button

✔ Property details submitted


Button: View

🔥 This checklist replaces 10 tooltips, emails, and support tickets.

3️⃣ PROPERTY CARD — LIMITED MODE (NO ACTION LIES)

⚠️ IMPORTANT:
Disabled buttons must show tooltip:

“Available after approval”

This avoids rage clicks.

4️⃣ “WHAT HAPPENS NEXT” EXPLAINER (SHORT, HUMAN)

A simple info card:

Title

What happens next?

Text (keep this exact tone)
• Our team reviews your details (24–48 hrs)
• Once approved, your property goes live
• You’ll start receiving booking requests
• Payments are released after guest check-in


This sets expectations and reduces WhatsApp spam.

5️⃣ SUPPORT & ESCALATION (MUST EXIST)

At the bottom or side:

Need help?
📞 Call / WhatsApp Support
📩 Raise a ticket


Why?

Pre-approval owners are nervous

This prevents drop-offs + abuse on social media

6️⃣ WHAT MUST NOT APPEAR ON THIS SCREEN ❌

Do NOT show:

Earnings

Bookings

Analytics

Ratings

Reviews

Promotional tools

Even placeholders are bad.
Fake dashboards destroy trust.

7️⃣ PERMISSION LOGIC (IMPLEMENT THIS EXACTLY)
if (owner.status === 'pending' || owner.status === 'under_review') {
  showPreApprovalDashboard();
  disable([
    'pricing',
    'bookings',
    'payouts',
    'visibility'
  ]);
}

8️⃣ NAVIGATION — LIMITED MENU
Sidebar / Top nav (pre-approval)

Show ONLY:

Dashboard

My Property

Documents / KYC

Support

Logout

Hide:

Bookings

Earnings

Reviews

Messages (optional)

9️⃣ COPY THAT YOU SHOULD USE (DON’T OVERWRITE)

Use plain, reassuring language.
Avoid “compliance”, “verification”, “KYC” jargon in headlines.

Say:

“Get live”

“Start receiving bookings”

“We’re reviewing”
